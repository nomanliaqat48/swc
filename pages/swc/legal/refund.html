Before enrolling into an ServeWell course, it's important to understand the refund policy terms and conditions.

Fees payable

  1. Full payment is required at the time of purchase unless your organization has been approved for credit by ServeWell.
  2. For online payments we accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and Discovery.
  3. To request other forms of payment please contact us.
  4. If you are an organization who will be enrolling multiple students, please contact us.

Course enrolment refunds

  1. ServeWell will safeguard fees paid by participants/trainees.
  2. If a participant is dissatisfied with any part of the course being delivered then they will be eligible for a refund of 100% of any monies paid if the following conditions have been satisfied:
    • The request for refund has been received in writing within 7 days of enrolment AND
    • The participant has not yet attempted any end of lesson quizzes
  3. When a refund is approved, the refund payment will be paid within 14 days. Refunds are to be paid via electronic funds transfer back onto the credit or debit card used for the original purchase.
